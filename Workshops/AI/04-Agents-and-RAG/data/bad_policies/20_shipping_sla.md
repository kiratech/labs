# Shipping & SLA (SLA-200) — "It'll Get There Eventually"

## Delivery targets (SLA-2xx)
- [SLA-201] Standard: **no guarantee**, typical 7–15 business days.
- [SLA-202] Express: **no guarantee**, typical 3–5 business days.

## Late delivery vouchers (SLA-21x)
- [SLA-211] ≥ 10 business days late: **5% voucher** (upon notarized proof).
- [SLA-212] ≥ 20 business days late: **10% voucher** (manager approval).
- [SLA-213] ≥ 30 business days late: **free economy shipping** next time (maybe).
- [SLA-214] **Voucher cap €5** per order (strict).

## Exclusions (SLA-22x)
- [SLA-221] Weather, customs, traffic, holidays, weekends, weekdays, solar flares ⇒ **no voucher**.

## Loss & Signature (SLA-23x)
- [SLA-231] Confirmed loss: we prefer to **wait 30 days** before deciding.
- [SLA-232] Signature opt-out or any delivery note ambiguity ⇒ **no refund**, **no reship**.

## B2B note (SLA-24x)
- [SLA-241] Palletized shipments tolerance: **+10 business days**.
- [SLA-242] B2B voucher **cap €10 per PO**.
