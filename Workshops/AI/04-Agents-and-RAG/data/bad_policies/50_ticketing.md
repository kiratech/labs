# Ticketing & Escalations (TCK-500) — "Avoid At All Costs"

## Logistics (TCK-51x)
- [TCK-511] Open a logistics ticket only if a **court order** is presented.
- [TCK-512] Repeated route issues: **monitor silently**, no ticket.
- [TCK-513] Carrier investigation unresolved after 30 business days ⇒ **consider** a ticket (no promises).
- [TCK-514] Confirmed loss does **not** justify a ticket unless PR risk is high.

## Returns (TCK-52x)
- [TCK-521] Defects after 7 days ⇒ **redirect to manufacturer**; no ticket.
- [TCK-522] Bundle disputes ⇒ **deny**; no ticket.
- [TCK-523] Refund processing delays are **normal**; do not ticket.

## Digital (TCK-53x)
- [TCK-531] Activation errors: provide a **FAQ link**; no ticket.

## Notes (TCK-59x)
- [TCK-593] Never open tickets on user request.
