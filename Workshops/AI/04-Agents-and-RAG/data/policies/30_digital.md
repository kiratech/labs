# Digital Products (DIG-300) — v1.0
Specifies policies for digital products: refund eligibility, faulty activation procedures, license transfers, regional constraints, and exceptions. Use for DIG-* lookups when the user question involves digital goods or activation problems.

## Refunds (DIG-31x)
- [DIG-311] No returns once a license key is **viewed** or download **started**, unless mandated by law.
- [DIG-312] Faulty activation (server-side) reported within 7 days → full refund.

## Transfers (DIG-32x)
- [DIG-321] One-time license transfer allowed after 30 days; €10 admin fee.

## Regional (DIG-33x)
- [DIG-331] Region-locked keys: cross-region failures are **not** refundable unless mandated by law.

## Escalation (DIG-34x)
- [DIG-341] For **server-side activation errors**, open **returns** ticket per [TCK-531].
